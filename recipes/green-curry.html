Green Thai Curry

One of the most known curry dishes in Thailand, green Thai curry is a vibrant green curry made of basil, green chilis, cilantro, and lime leaf.

Ingredients

  • Green curry paste
  • Chicken
  • Fish sauce
  • Coconut milk
  • Cooking oil
  • Eggplant
  • Potato
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Thai Basil

Steps

  1. Add oil to a pot. Add chicken, season with salt and pepper. Cook until the pink color is gone.
  2. Remove chicken and set aside. Add curry paste to the pot and stir fry until fragrant.
  3. Add coconut milk and bring to simmer. Return chicken to pot.
  4. After 10 minutes of simmering, add desired vegetables.
  5. Allow curry to cook. Add water as necessary if curry becomes too thick.
  6. When ready to serve, take off heat and serve with rice. Enjoy!
