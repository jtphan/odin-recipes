Ca Kho To (Vietnamese Braised Catfish)

Ca Kho To is a traditional Vietnamese comfort dish. It is a braished fish dish that is caramalized in a very savory sauce. The fish is cooked to be very tender and fragrant and pairs well with plain white rice.

Ingredients

  • Fish (usually Catfish)
  • Thai Chili
  • Cooking oil
  • Sugar
  • Fish sauce
  • Garlic
  • Shallots

Steps

  1. Combine fish with sugar, fish sauce, oil, garlic, and shallots. Leave to marinate
  2. On low heat, add oil and sugar to start the caramel.
  3. Pour in water to the pot and add the fish. Turn heat high and let fish simmer
  4. Allow fish to braise over low-medium heat or until soft and tender. Sauce should become thick and sticky.
  5. Add chili for additional spiciness.
  6. Serve with steam white rice and enjoy!
