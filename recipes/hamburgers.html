Hamburgers

This is a tasty hamburger.

A hamburger is probably one of the most iconic foods in American culture. It consists of a nice hearty bun, a hefty beefy patty, and a assortment of vegetables and condiments. In my most recent explorations of hamburger varieties, I have seen them with thick, juicy patties, or even as a "smash" burger. Burgers come in all shapes and sizes.

Ingredients

  • Brioche Bun or Potato roll
  • Ground beef (chuck, sirloin, short rib, brisket)
  • Butter
  • Lettuce (iceberg or butter)
  • Onion (grilled or raw)
  • Tomato
  • Mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard
  • Dill pickles
  • Cheese of chioce (American or cheddar)

Steps

    Recipe inspired from Kenji Lopez Alt's

  1. Form ground beef into patties of desired size and shape. Season all sides with salt and pepper
  2. Add butter to a skillet and heat to medium high. Add patties and cook until a nice crust forms. Low heat as necessary
  3. Flip the burgers and top with cheese as necessary. Cook until browned. Use thermometer to read 125 for medium rare
  4. Transfer patties onto plate. Transfer buns into the skillet and toast to desirability
  5. Assemble vegetables and condiments onto bun and construct the burger.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
